About Us

CCH: A Legacy of Football Since 1965

Club Chabab Houara, founded in 1965 in Houara, Morocco, is a historic football club now competing in the Moroccan third division GNFA 1. With a legacy of passion and determination, we continue to represent our community with pride in black and yellow.
